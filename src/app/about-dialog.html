The following guidelines are used to determine what elements are included in the Lego Shield Collection:

  1. Any element that was initially designed to be held by a minifig as a shield. Accounts for the inclusion of 75902pb19 even though it is used as a vehicle decal in a Technic set.
  2. Any element that is held by a minifig as a shield. Accounts for the inclusion of 98834pb02 even though it was introduced as a vehicle spoiler.
  3. Brick built shields are not included. Exceptions are made when a single tile is added to a shield element (14769pb523), as in the Goat Boat (76208) set.
  4. Offensive weapons that are used to block an attack are not included. This eliminates swords, light sabers and power burst shields.
  5. Nexo Knight shield tiles are not included. The four Nexo Knight shields are included, but the 161 Nexo Knight shield tiles that attach are not included (see Nexo Collection).
  6. Lego duplo and bionicle shields are not included.
